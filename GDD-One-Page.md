# The Long Night - One-Page GDD

## High Concept
Top-down pixel-art stealth action RPG set on the ark ship Khepri.  
You play Nick Wallace, a Noctis survivor balancing protection, predation, and identity while uncovering an ancient war.

## Pillars
- Tension in darkness: visibility, sound, and alarms shape every encounter.
- Surgical action: short, lethal fights with positioning and cooldown timing.
- Moral pressure: choices trade immediate survival for long-term trust/outcomes.
- Ancient mystery: memory bleed events reveal Old One history and future threats.

## Audience and Platform
- Audience: players who enjoy narrative sci-fi, stealth-action loops, and retro visuals.
- Initial platform target: PC.
- Input target: keyboard/mouse first, controller-compatible.

## Core Loop
1. Enter sector.
2. Complete critical tasks (power, life support, security).
3. Avoid or eliminate threats.
4. Resolve narrative decision.
5. Return to hub and unlock deeper deck.

## Gameplay Systems (MVP)
- 8-direction movement.
- Interaction/tasks.
- Stealth detection meter (line-of-sight + noise).
- Light melee combat (strike, dodge, short cooldowns).
- Puzzle states (power rerouting, lock sequencing).
- Persistent narrative flags.

## Visual and Audio Direction
- Art: 2D top-down pixel art, 320x180 internal resolution.
- Palette: cold blues/blacks, blood-red highlights, infrared UI overlays.
- Camera: top-down with slight perspective feel.
- Audio: low mechanical ambience, sparse impacts, dynamic danger pulse.

## Narrative Setup
Earth is gone. The Noctis crew of Khepri carries human embryos toward survival.  
Old One technology transforms the mission from ark to weapon.  
Wallace's memory bleed links him to the first war and what still waits in The Wound.

## Intro Level (Cold Wake - Deck A)
- Tutorial goal: teach movement, interaction, stealth, combat, puzzle logic, and first moral choice.
- Completion target: 10-15 minutes.
- End hook: Ari reveals a signal tied to old war wreckage beyond Deck A.

## Success Criteria
- First-time players can finish intro without external explanation.
- Players correctly understand stealth vs direct combat tradeoff.
- End-of-level choice feels meaningful and is stored for later payoff.

## Production Scope (Vertical Slice)
- Build one complete playable level (Deck A).
- Include one enemy type, one puzzle type, one major narrative branch.
- Deliver polished art/audio pass for this level only.
