# Timeline

## The First War (Ancient History)

An advanced civilization known as the Old Ones fought a war against the Enemy.

Key facts:

- warfare included reality-distorting technologies
- psychological warfare played a central role
- the conflict severely damaged regions of space

Eventually the Old Ones disappeared, likely destroying themselves before defeat.

---

## Creation of the Noctis

Thousands of years later, humanity began experimenting with extreme biological modification to enable interstellar travel.

The Noctis program was born.

Volunteers were transformed into beings capable of surviving deep space without conventional life support.

---

## Collapse of Earth

Environmental collapse and societal breakdown pushed humanity toward extinction.

A final mission was created:

The **Khepri Ark Project**.

Purpose:
carry frozen human embryos to a distant habitable world.

---

## Launch of the Khepri

A secret launch sends the ark ship into deep space.

The Noctis crew enters long-duration hibernation cycles as the journey begins.

---

## Early Voyage

Routine rotations maintain the ship.

Strange signals and unexplained phenomena begin appearing during the voyage.

Wallace experiences memory bleed connected to ancient events.

---

## Discovery of Old One Hulk

The Khepri encounters a derelict Old One war vessel.

Recovered technologies allow the ship to transform from an ark into a weapon.

---

## The Proving Ground

The crew tests recovered technologies on an ancient training world.

Noctis combat transformations are developed during this phase.

The crew crosses a psychological threshold.

Doubt disappears.

Purpose becomes absolute.

---

## First Ambush

The Enemy attempts to trap the Khepri using a distress signal.

The trap is reversed.

The Khepri destroys the ambushing fleet using stealth infiltration and decapitation strikes.

---

## The Wound

Signals lead the crew toward a damaged region of space left from the first war.

Reality behaves unpredictably there.

The Khepri begins to uncover the deeper origins of the conflict between the Old Ones and the Enemy.

The war that destroyed the Old Ones may not be finished.
