# Characters

## Nick Wallace

Role: Protagonist

Nick Wallace is one of the original volunteers who underwent conversion into a Noctis in order to crew the ark ship Khepri.

Before conversion he was terminally ill. His transformation was more difficult than others and left him altered in ways that are not fully understood.

### Traits
- strategic thinker
- emotionally restrained
- carries memories that are not his own

### Unique Condition
Wallace appears to carry genetic or neurological traces of the **Old One** lineage.

This causes:

- memory bleed from ancient wars
- instinctive understanding of Old One systems
- compatibility with technologies others cannot activate

Over time Wallace begins to evolve beyond standard Noctis physiology.

---

## Ari

Role: Tactical and philosophical counterbalance to Wallace

Ari is one of the most stable and capable members of the Noctis crew.

She becomes a central leader among both the Noctis and the hybrid children.

### Traits
- calm under extreme pressure
- strategic and analytical
- emotionally disciplined

### Development
Ari increasingly acts as the bridge between:

- the Noctis
- the hybrid children
- Wallace

Her understanding of the larger conflict grows faster than most of the crew.

---

## The Hybrid Children

Role: Strategic intelligence of the Khepri

The children originate from human embryos carried aboard the ark.

Some develop hybrid traits due to Old One genetic influence.

### Characteristics
- rapid intellectual development
- emotional detachment
- strong collective awareness
- natural interface with ancient technology

They rarely argue or disagree.

Instead they behave like different expressions of a single distributed mind.

### Role in the Story
They gradually become the decision-making core of the Khepri.

---

## The Noctis Crew

Role: Soldiers, guardians, and executioners

The Noctis crew were once human volunteers who underwent transformation to survive deep space travel.

### Shared Traits
- long lifespan
- extreme physical durability
- predatory reflexes
- infrared vision

After the proving ground trials, some undergo additional transformation into **combat war forms**.

### Noctis War Forms
Enhancements include:

- bonded armor fused to flesh
- cloaking capability
- neural tendrils for combat and energy extraction
- silent vacuum propulsion

These transformed Noctis become the primary boarding and infiltration force of the Khepri.

---

## The Old One

Role: Ancient precursor entity connected to Wallace

The Old One is an ancient hybrid being from the precursor civilization that fought the Enemy.

Fragments of its genetic or neurological pattern appear to exist within Wallace.

It represents:

- the original design of the Noctis concept
- a link to the first war
- knowledge lost to history
