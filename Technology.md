# Technology

## Ark Ship Khepri

Original role: interstellar ark carrying human embryos.

Structure:

- enormous skeletal structure resembling a floating skyscraper
- modular internal architecture
- industrial fabrication systems
- biological interface systems

Lighting and displays operate primarily in the infrared spectrum to suit Noctis perception.

---

## Stasis System

Embryos are preserved in advanced cryogenic storage.

The crew uses a different system:

### Hibernation Stasis

- metabolic slowdown
- neurological stabilization
- periodic waking rotations for maintenance

This allows the crew to survive journeys lasting centuries.

---

## Old One Technologies

Recovered from derelict warships and installations.

Examples include:

### Combat Automata
- non-sentient war machines
- designed for boarding and close combat
- extremely durable and efficient

### Living Armor
- biological-metal hybrid systems
- bond directly to host physiology
- enhance speed, strength, and survivability

### Exotic Weapons
Examples recovered by the Khepri:

- molecular disintegration cannons
- phase piercing spike arrays
- spacetime shear weapons capable of tearing localized reality

---

## Boarding Pods

Small stealth craft used for infiltration.

Features:

- minimal emissions
- cold drift approach
- capable of carrying automata or personnel

The Noctis use them in unconventional ways, often clinging to the hull rather than traveling inside.

---

## Noctis War Form Enhancements

Used during later stages of the story.

Enhancements include:

- organic armor bonded to skeletal structure
- cloaking systems
- neural tendrils capable of penetrating ships and organisms
- extraction of biological fluids to enhance combat performance
- vacuum propulsion systems enabling silent maneuvering in space

These modifications transform Noctis into highly lethal boarding specialists.

---

## Reality Distortion Weapons

The most dangerous Old One weapons recovered.

Capabilities include:

- localized spacetime rupture
- gravitational shear
- matter annihilation

These weapons are used sparingly due to the unpredictable effects on surrounding space.
